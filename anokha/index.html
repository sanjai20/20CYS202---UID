INTRODUCTION


Anokha Techfest, the annual event of Amrita School of Engineering, Coimbatore provides an unique opportunity
for students to participate in fun-filled and challenging events and workshops. The event throws open opportunities for students from
all disciplines to challenge themselves and foster the “Can Do” attitude.
One of the key highlights of the Anokha 2022 edition was the Green Anokha concept.
It was so wonderful to see the students adopt and implement our Chancellor AMMA's idea of “Living in Harmony with Nature”.
It was plastic-free and eco-friendly Anokha-an initiative that will become a tradition!!


ABOUT ANOKHA



Anokha, the national engineering techfest of Amrita School of Engineering, Coimbatore, is a 3-day congregation of some of the brightest minds in India. Founded in 2010, Anokha has grown by leaps and bounds and has progressed to become one of the most popular and top techfests in India. Having successfully completed eight editions, Anokha has had an average annual participation of over 10,000 of the best undergraduate engineering students from top-ranking engineering institutions in India like IITs, BITS, NITs and IIITs participating as well as partner universities in USA and Europe like University of New Mexico, EVRY France and Uppsala University-Sweden.

Anokha provides a unique opportunity for the student community to showcase their talents and skills and also provides a forum for the industry and the academia to interact with each other. The event witnesses the coming together of the best minds from across India and abroad to participate in a wide array of technical and non-technical events and workshops. The event is a perfect platform for the students to display their creativity, technical expertise and innovative ideas. It also provides an opportunity for the industry to identify and encourage talented young minds. Anokha is truly a celebration of knowledge, innovation and creativity.

ABOUT

Anokha is an annual inter-college technical fest organized by Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham. It is a three-day event which provides a platform for students to showcase their technical and creative skills. The fest includes several technical events, workshops, lectures and competitions, which test and challenge the participants' knowledge and skills in the field of engineering and technology. Anokha is an ideal platform to nurture and hone the technical skills of students and to provide them with an opportunity to interact with experts in the field.
